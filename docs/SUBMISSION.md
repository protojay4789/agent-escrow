## 🧠 + 🛡️ THE KILLER COMBO: Brain × Enforcement

**This is our moat.** Nobody else has this.

### The Problem With Every Other Agent Platform

They give you a brain. You hope it doesn't blow up your wallet.

- "Here's GPT-4. Good luck."
- No guardrails. No progressive unlocking. No accountability.
- User deposits $10K → agent loses it in an hour → platform shrugs.

### Our Solution: Brain Intelligence + Governance Control

**Layer 1 (Brain)** decides what to do.
**Layer 6 (Enforcement)** decides what it's ALLOWED to do.

The brain can be as aggressive or conservative as you want — but the enforcement layer is the **circuit breaker** that fires regardless of confidence.

```
Brain: "This trade will 10x. I'm 99% sure."
Enforcement: "You're a Student. Max 5% position. Rejected."

Brain: "I see an arbitrage opportunity."
Enforcement: "Daily loss limit hit. Cooling off for 6 hours."

Brain: "Let me try again."
Enforcement: "You're at Tier 2. 5-minute cooldown between trades."
```

### Progressive Unlocking — Education Meets Governance

| Tier | Unlocks | How |
|------|---------|-----|
| 🟢 Student | Tight limits, whitelisted protocols only | Enroll in Academy |
| 🟡 Graduate | Moderate limits, expanded whitelist | Complete courses |
| 🔴 Auditor | Loose limits, custom protocols | Prove audit skills |
| ⚫ Architect | Custom enforcement rules | Proven track record |

**The flywheel:**
1. User enrolls in PGE Academy (Layer 3 — Strategy education)
2. Completes modules → earns REP (Layer 8 — Lifecycle)
3. REP unlocks higher enforcement tiers (Layer 6 — Governance)
4. Higher tiers → more aggressive strategies (Layer 3 — Strategy)
5. Better strategies → higher leaderboard rankings (Layer 5 — Social)
6. Rankings → more visibility → more revenue (Layer 8 — Economics)

**No other platform connects education → governance → execution → revenue in a single composable stack.**
